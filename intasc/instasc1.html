InTASC Standard #1

The teacher understands how learners grow and develop, recognizing that patterns of learning and development vary individually within and across the cognitive, linguistic, social, emotional, and physical areas, and designs and implements developmentally appropriate and challenging learning experiences.

High School students come to computer programming classes with different levels of programming, motivations, and learning rates. Along with theory, demonstrations, and coding assignments I provide mentoring, feedback, and significant scaffolding as needed. Some need little more than the coding opportunity while others struggle and need a lot of personal attention.
