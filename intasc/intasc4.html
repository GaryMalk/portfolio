InTASC Standard #4
Content Knowledge

The teacher understands the central concepts, tools of inquiry, and structures of the discipline(s) he or she teaches and creates learning experiences that make the discipline accessible and meaningful for learners to assure mastery of the content.

This standard is self-explanitory: I'm expected to be an expert in the content I teach. I demonstrate my knowledge and skills through lectures that are comprehensible and expand a student's knowledge, easy-to-follow demonstrations, answering questions, and mentoring students.

My field is computer science, so just as important is transmitting the skill to seek out knowledge and apply it. IT moves so fast that no one can possibly know it all - certainly not me - so beyond the basics, the real skill needed in industry isn't what you know but how fast you can learn. I transmit this skill by honestly answering, when appropriate, "I don't know, let's find out" and assisting a student researching a topic or technique.
